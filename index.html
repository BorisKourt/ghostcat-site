What is Ghost.Cat?

Ghost.Cat is a theme for the Ghost blogging platform. Ghost started as a wildly successful kickstarted campaign and is now available to the backers.

As a Ghost theme Ghost.Cat is strongly focused on blogging and nothing else. Just like the platform this theme will be under constant development and anyone who owns a copy of it will receive updates for free.

What can it do?

As mentioned above Ghost.Cat is a no frills blogging theme. Each post is based around the idea of 'cards' and acts as a self contained node. The theme is also fully responsive and adjusts from single column to multi column for larger resolutions.

Why should I buy this?

A great deal of time was spent in refining the aesthetics of this theme. The hard work put into that is what we are banking on. This theme is meant to last, as not only are you going to receive constant improvements it is also designed to be very future proof.

What about further customization?

As ghost progresses you will see a great number of improvements happen over-time. Their theme development features will be growing and so will the options provided by Ghost.Cat
